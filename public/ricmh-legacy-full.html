About the Conference

International Congress on Metabolic Health 2026

The Riyadh International Congress on Metabolic Health brings together leading clinicians, researchers, and healthcare professionals to explore the latest advancements in metabolic diseases.

Through expert-led sessions, scientific discussions, and evidence-based insights, the congress aims to address the growing challenges of diabetes, obesity, and cardiometabolic disorders.

Participants will gain practical clinical knowledge, engage with international experts, and contribute to shaping the future of metabolic medicine in the region.
